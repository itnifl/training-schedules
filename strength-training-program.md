## Notes

- **Warm-up**: 5-10 minutes of cardio + dynamic stretching before each session
- **Progressive Overload**: Increase weight when you can complete all reps with good form
- **Rest Days**: Allow at least 48 hours before training the same muscle group
- **Nutrition**: Prioritize protein intake (1.6-2.2g per kg of bodyweight)
- **Hydration**: Drink water throughout workouts
- **AMRAP**: As Many Reps As Possible
